Evidence #2

  1. TTS, or Topsport Talent Schools, is founded in Netherlands in 1991. This research studied the effect of attending a TTS than a mainstream schools. From their particpatiants of former talented athletes from six sport federations they concluded a couple of points.
    a. The lack of increased sport performance levels of talented athletes who attended TTS could be explained by the fact that there are rarely sport specific training facilities available at TTS (Flatau and Emrich, 2011).
    b. Based on the theory that less time spent at school leads to less school bonding and lower school motivation, talented athletes who attended TTS are expected to have achieved a lower school performance level compared to those at mainstream secondary schools.
